How to play?

This interactive tutorial is designed for users totally new to cybersecurity or programming. The assessment is aimed for learners to understand the basic concepts of cybersecurity. We used blockly, a block based programming language to help inexperienced users to learn cybersecurity effectively

Step 1

Step 2

Step 3
