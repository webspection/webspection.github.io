How does firewall works?

Firewall will examine messages entering or leaving the private network and block message that do not meet our security requirements. Firewall is designed to protect our network traffic and connections, it is a front line defense of our private information


How does a firewall provide protection?

Firewall can prevent malicious software or network connection to access our private network or personal computer. We can configure our firewall to block data with certain ports or network locations
