Why do we need a strong password?

Strong password provides essential protection from brute force attack. Hackers will try to break into our account by guessing passwords. Strong password can prevent attackers to gain accesss and control of our accounts


What is a brute force attack?

Attacker will try every possible combination of letters, numbers, or special characters or uses an automated script to try different combinations to guess your account credential
